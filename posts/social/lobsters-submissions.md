## Submission 1: JAPL

**Title:** JAPL: A Self-Hosting FP Language Compiling to TypeScript and C

**URL:** https://japl-nine.vercel.app

**Tags:** compilers, plt, functional, typescript

**Description (for comment):**

JAPL is a statically-typed, pure-by-default functional language with Erlang-style concurrency and Rust-inspired resource safety. The compiler is hand-written (no parser generators), targets both TypeScript and C, and can compile itself (1,495 lines of self-hosting JAPL).

Backed by 7 research papers covering the type system, process model, compiler architecture, and self-hosting. All open source.

Source: https://github.com/YonedaAI/japl

## Submission 2: The Yoneda Constraint
